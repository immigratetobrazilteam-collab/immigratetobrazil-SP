Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our policy on service cancellations and refunds.

Cancellation Within 7 Days

You may cancel services within 7 days of payment for a full refund, provided no work has commenced on your case.

After Work Begins

Once our team has begun work on your case (document review, application preparation), a partial refund is issued for unused services (minimum 25% non-refundable processing fee).

Case-Specific Terms

Some services have specific terms: Consultation fees are non-refundable after consultation is provided, Application services are non-refundable after submission, Representation services include a retainer that applies to case costs.

Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 14 business days of approval. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing fees may apply for international refunds.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable: Government application fees already submitted, Travel/accommodation costs, Third-party services already paid, Work completed on your case.

Partial Refunds

For services cancelled mid-engagement, refunds are calculated as: Total fees - work completed value - 25% processing fee = refund amount.

Service Suspension

We reserve the right to suspend services if payment is 30+ days overdue. Services resume upon full payment of outstanding balance plus any late fees.

Request a Refund

Submit refund requests in writing to refunds@immigratetobrazil.com with your case number and reason for cancellation.
