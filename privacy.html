Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Immigrate to Brazil handles your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone)
  • Immigration details (visa type, current status)
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Communication records with our team

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Process your immigration case
  • Communicate about your application
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send you important updates (with consent)

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access. Your data is protected under Brazilian data protection laws and international standards.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We only share information with:

  • Government immigration agencies (as required)
  • Service providers under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred for immigration processing. We ensure it's protected under equivalent data protection standards.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@immigratetobrazil.com
