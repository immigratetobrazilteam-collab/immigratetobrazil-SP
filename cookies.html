Cookie Policy

Information about how we use cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and remember your preferences. We use cookies to improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (login sessions, security). Always active.
Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how you use our site to help us improve it.
Preference Cookies: Remember your settings (language choice, layout preferences).
Marketing Cookies: Track your activity for targeted advertising (only with consent).

Third-Party Cookies

We use analytics tools (Google Analytics) that set cookies. These are governed by third parties' privacy policies. You can control these in your browser settings.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings: Accept/decline cookies, Delete existing cookies, Set preferences for specific sites. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being set.

Disabling Cookies

Disabling all cookies may affect website functionality. Some features (login, form submission) require cookies to work properly.

Cookie Duration

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for specified periods (typically 1-12 months).

Do Not Track

If your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal, we respect this preference and limit cookie collection for non-essential purposes.

Updates to Policy

We may update this cookie policy. Check back periodically for changes. Continued use of our website indicates acceptance of updated policies.
