Data Protection & GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR and Brazilian data protection regulations (LGPD).

Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to: (1) Access your data, (2) Request corrections, (3) Request erasure, (4) Restrict processing, (5) Data portability, (6) Object to processing, (7) Lodge complaints with authorities.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on: Contract performance, Legal obligation, Legitimate interests, Your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary: Case files retained for 7 years (legal requirement), Correspondence retained for 3 years, Marketing data deleted upon request.

Data Protection Officer

Our Data Protection Officer oversees GDPR compliance. Contact: dpo@immigratetobrazil.com

International Data Transfers

Data transfers outside Brazil/EU use Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure adequate protection.

Breach Notification

If we discover a data breach, we'll notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by law.

Third-Party Processors

All processors sign Data Processing Agreements ensuring GDPR compliance and data security.

Brazilian LGPD Compliance

We also comply with Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), respecting rights similar to GDPR for all clients.
