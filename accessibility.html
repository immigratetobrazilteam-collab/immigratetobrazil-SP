Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making our website accessible to all users.

Our Commitment

We strive to provide a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities. We comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards.

Keyboard Navigation

Our website is fully navigable using keyboard. Press Tab to navigate, Enter to select. Use Shift+M to skip to main content and Shift+T to scroll to top.

Screen Reader Support

All content is structured with semantic HTML and ARIA labels for screen reader compatibility. Images include descriptive alt text.

Color & Contrast

We use sufficient color contrast ratios (at least 4.5:1 for text) to ensure readability for people with low vision.

Text & Font

We use readable fonts and allow text resizing. No information is conveyed by color alone.

Forms

All form fields are properly labeled and marked as required where applicable. Error messages are clear and helpful.

Video & Audio

Videos include captions and transcripts. Audio content has transcripts available.

Report Issues

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at accessibility@immigratetobrazil.com. We will work to resolve issues promptly.
